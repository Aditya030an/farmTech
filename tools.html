Common Farm Tools List and Equipment Name with Uses and Picture

Tractors

Tractors can be used to do a variety of tasks, including plowing, tilling, harvesting, hauling, and more.

Rakes

It is used to break up and remove clumps of soil, spread fertilizer, smooth out soil, remove debris, and prepare the ground for planting by removing weeds and loosening the soil

Fertilizer Spreaders

Fertilizer spreaders are an indispensable tool in modern agriculture, providing farmers with a cost-effective, efficient, and precise way to apply fertilizer to their land.

Seeders

Seeders are used to sow and spread seeds in a neat, efficient manner. They can be used for a variety of crops, including vegetables, grains, and forage, and can be used for both small and large acreage farming operations.

Sprinklers

Sprinklers are used to irrigate (water) agricultural crops, lawns, landscapes, golf courses, and other areas. Aside from cooling, they also control dust in the air.

Hay Balers

It is a piece of farm machinery used to compress crop material (such as hay, cotton, flax straw, salt marsh hay, or silage) into compact bales.

Hoes

Agricultural and horticultural hand tools such as hoes are used to shape soil, remove weeds, clear soil, and harvest roots.

Shovels

Shovels are used for digging, lifting, and moving bulk materials, such as dirt, coal, gravel, sand, and snow.
